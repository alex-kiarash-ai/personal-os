# Venture Sync (utility, unnumbered)

## What it actually does
On demand (`/venture-sync`): mirrors business-venture documents - plans, notes, decks living outside the system - into the vault under vault/ventures/, converts them into wiki pages with links, generates briefs, and scaffolds project pages so a venture's knowledge joins the same graph as everything else.

## Why it exists
STEMPLICITY and other venture ideas generate documents in scattered places; knowledge that isn't in the vault is invisible to every automation (the brief can't mention it, research can't cite it, the CRM can't connect people to it). This is the on-ramp that brings venture material into the system's memory.

## Works together with
- **The vault** - its entire output surface (vault/ventures/ + cross-links).
- **[Research Team](04-research-team.md)** and **[Content Machine](09-content-machine.md)** - downstream consumers of synced venture knowledge.
