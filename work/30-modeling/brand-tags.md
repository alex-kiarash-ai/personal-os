# Brand-tag allowlist (B1 flywheel)

The caption step may tag ONLY brands on this list. Hard cap **1-2 tags per post**, and only when the item is genuinely worn/used in the frame. The play (B1): genuine tags put the account in front of exactly the people who commission UGC; detection of brand interactions rides the Phase-2 warm-lead flagger, until then the manual engagement ritual catches them.

Rules:
- A tag earns its row here ONLY from real wear: Shaheen owns it, wears it on camera, would say so if asked.
- Never tag a brand to fish. One genuine tag beats five aspirational ones (and aspirational tagging reads as spam to both the algorithm and the brand's social team).
- A brand that interacts (like/comment/repost/story-share) -> hand-reported via a `teach-alex` note ("lead: ...") or caught by the Phase-2 flagger -> `warm-brand` row in the Modeling Leads ledger.
- Review at the monthly strategy run: drop dead rows, add new genuine wear.
