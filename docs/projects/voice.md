# Voice (unnumbered, parked)

## What it actually does
A two-way, interruptible voice conversation with Alex, running locally: press Enter and talk (recording stops when you go quiet), local Whisper turns speech to text, one persistent Claude process (the full Alex brain: soul.md, the vault, the tools, loaded once) answers, and the reply is spoken sentence by sentence so it starts fast and can be cut off mid-sentence with Escape. Speech never leaves the machine; the voice out is OpenAI's TTS when credits exist, with a free built-in Windows voice as automatic fallback so it never goes silent.

## Why it exists
Typing is a bottleneck for half the thoughts, and the HQ note-drop is one-way. This is the conversational lane: same brain, no keyboard.

## Current state: PARKED (2026-07-05, revisit 2026-08-01)
Built from a research run, launch bugs fixed and verified end to end, then parked the same night at Shaheen's call: the voice quality was not good enough yet. Parked cleanly, with a Notion row holding full resume context. The revisit starts with the specific gripe (which voice, what latency), not a rebuild.
