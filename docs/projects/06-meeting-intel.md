# 06 — Meeting Intel

## What it actually does
Two jobs, on demand. **Before** a meeting: builds a dossier on whoever Shaheen is meeting — what the vault knows, what the CRM knows, what the public web adds. **After**: drop any artifact of the meeting into its inbox folder — typed notes, a PDF, a transcript, even an audio recording (transcribed locally with Whisper) — and it produces structured notes, pulls out the action items onto the sprint board, updates the people pages, and drafts the follow-up message.

## Why it exists
The value of a meeting leaks away within a day if nothing captures it. This makes capture a drag-and-drop: any file format in, structured memory + actions + follow-up out. The pre-meeting dossier exists because walking in knowing more than expected is the cheapest advantage there is — especially in interviews and client calls during a pivot.

## Works together with
- **[Personal CRM](05-personal-crm.md)** — dossiers read from it; meeting outcomes update it.
- **[Sprint Tracker](01-sprint-tracker.md)** — action items land on its board.
- **[Morning Brief](02-morning-brief.md)** — flags upcoming meetings that deserve a dossier.
- **The vault** — meetings become vault/meetings/ pages linked to people and companies.
